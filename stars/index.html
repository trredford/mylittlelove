EACH PAGE SHOWS A DIFFERENT IDEA OF WHAT HAPPENS AFTER WE DIE, AND HOW REGARDLESS OF WHAT HAPPENS, I WILL ALWAYS BE THERE FOR MY DAUGHTER. EACH PAGE HAS AN INTERACTIVE ELEMENT, REQUESTING THAT THE USER PARTICIPATE AND REFLECT ON THE MANY THEMES OF DEATH. WHAT DO YOU BELIEVE HAPPENS AFTER WE DIE? DO YOU THINK WE WILL RECONNECT WITH THOSE WE LOVE, IN WHATEVER MANNER THAT MAY BE?
